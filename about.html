About Us

Unetra HR Solutions Established in 2019 by a team of seasoned HR managers, Unetra HR Solutions has become a trusted name in recruitment and HR consulting. We specialize in delivering top-quality recruitment services across Insurance, EdTech, Pharma, BFSI, and Real Estate industries.

Trusted, 5+ Years Experienced & Specialists

With over 5+ years of experience, Unetra is a trusted manpower consulting firm specializing in providing end-to-end staffing solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of businesses across various industries. We are dedicated to delivering the right talent to help organizations achieve their strategic goals, drive innovation, and enhance operational efficiency.

Our strength lies in providing high-quality candidates who not only meet job requirements but also align with your company’s culture and values. We prioritize building long-term partnerships with both clients and candidates by offering personalized, tailored solutions.
